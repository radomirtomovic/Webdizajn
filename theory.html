Harmony

Harmony of jazz is based on harmony of classical music. The main difference classical music theory and jazz theory is while classical music uses mostly uses triples, jazz almost exclusively uses sevenths. There are 11 types of seventh chords: Major 7 (maj7), dominant 7 (7), Minor-major (maj m7), minor 7 (m7), half-diminished 7 (ø7), diminished (dim7), augmented (aug7), sus
